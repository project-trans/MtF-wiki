Surgeon: **Prof. Kim Seok-Kwun (김석권)**

Institution: [Dong-A University Hospital](https://www.damc.or.kr/), Plastic Surgery / multidisciplinary clinic — Busan, South Korea.

## Overview

Kim Seok-Kwun performed Korea's first known MtF SRS in 1986 and is reported to have completed more than 320 gender-affirming surgeries — believed to be the highest individual count among Korean surgeons. He is often referred to as "the father of South Korean transgender medicine".[^kim-wiki][^transhealthcare]

Dong-A University Hospital operates a multidisciplinary gender clinic involving psychiatry, endocrinology, urology and plastic surgery, allowing assessment, HRT, surgery and follow-up under one roof.

## Techniques

- Penile-inversion vaginoplasty (primary)
- PI + scrotal flap combination
- Sigmoid colon vaginoplasty (in collaboration with general surgery / urology)

## Pre-operative Requirements (reference)

- Psychiatric diagnosis (typically F64.0 Gender Identity Disorder)
- Hormone therapy history (typically ≥ 1 year) and RLE recommended
- Pre-op workup: CBC, electrolytes, HIV, HBV, HCV, EKG, chest X-ray
- Guardian consent if under 19

Exact requirements vary by case — confirm at consultation.
