Surgeons: **Kim Jin-Hong (김진홍)** — urology / genito-urinary reconstructive surgery; **Kuylhee Kim (김퀼희)** — plastic surgery.[^transmap][^transhealthcare]

Institution: [Hallym University Kangdong Sacred Heart Hospital — LGBT+ Medical Center](https://www.kdh.or.kr/), Seoul (Gangdong-gu).

## Overview

As of recent reporting, Kangdong Sacred Heart is **the only general/university hospital in South Korea openly advertising LGBTQ+ medical services**.[^lgbt-health] Its LGBT+ Medical Center provides multidisciplinary care covering psychiatric evaluation, HRT, MtF and FtM surgeries, and post-operative follow-up.

- Kim Jin-Hong (Genito-Urinary Reconstructive Surgery Institute) — penile inversion, scrotal flap combination, and sigmoid colon vaginoplasty.
- Kuylhee Kim (Plastic Surgery) — breast augmentation and feminizing procedures.

## International Patients

An international patient department is available, with English-language support reported by patient directories. Direct written enquiry in advance is recommended.[^transmap]
