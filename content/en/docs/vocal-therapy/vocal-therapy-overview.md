## Overview {#overview}

Medically, voice feminization training falls within the scope of {{< wiki "speech therapy" >}}.

The English Wikipedia has a dedicated article on {{< wiki "Transgender voice therapy" en >}}.

## Surgery {#surgery}

After completing a structured course of voice feminization training, if you want to **raise pitch further**, you can consult a voice surgeon experienced in [voice feminization surgery]({{< ref "vocal-surgery-overview" >}}) to determine whether surgery is appropriate.

However, voice training is still needed after voice feminization surgery as a supplement, for example to adjust articulation and vocal presentation.

If you have already had voice feminization surgery, note that surgeries under general anesthesia may involve {{< wiki tracheal-intubation >}}. Be sure to confirm risks with an {{< wiki anesthesiologist >}} before surgery, for example before [SRS]({{< ref "srs-faq" >}}).
