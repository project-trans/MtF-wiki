## Route and dosage

{{< notice warning "Medication safety notice" >}}
Do not use if you are at high risk of breast cancer or have severe liver dysfunction.
{{< /notice >}}

- Route: [intramuscular injection](https://www.wikihow.com/Give-an-Intramuscular-Injection), [subcutaneous injection](https://www.wikihow.com/Give-a-Subcutaneous-Injection) {{< tag/neg "Do not inject if you do not know how." >}}
- Dosage:
  - Estradiol valerate: 5 mg every 5-7 days / 10 mg every 1-2 weeks
  - Estradiol cypionate: 5-6 mg every 7 days / 10-12 mg every 14 days
  - [Injectable estradiol simulator](https://transfemscience.org/misc/injectable-e2-simulator/)
- Notes:
  - Open ampoules [correctly](https://www.wikihow.com/Open-an-Ampule) to avoid glass injury, breakage, or glass particles in the solution.
  - Do not repeatedly inject the same site.
  - Avoid blood vessels. {{< tag/neg "May embolize vessels!" >}}
  - Avoid nerves. {{< tag/neg "Use a safe zone for intramuscular injection." >}}
  - Because these are oily preparations, a larger needle is often used to draw the medicine and an appropriate fresh needle is used for injection.
  - Fast needle insertion and changing to a fresh needle after drawing can reduce pain.
  - For self-injection, common sites include the vastus lateralis and deltoid.
  - If you feel severe pain during insertion or see a large blood return, remove the needle immediately and use a new needle and a new site.
  - More bleeding than usual after an injection is generally normal if it stops.
