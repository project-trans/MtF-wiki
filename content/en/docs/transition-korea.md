Below is a general flow of medical and legal transition steps in South Korea. None of them are strictly required; combine and reorder as fits your goals and circumstances.

## 1. Information & Self-Understanding

- Identify your goals (social, medical, legal) and priorities.
- Korean-language community and advocacy resources:
  - [Trans Roadmap](http://transroadmap.net/) — the most comprehensive Korean-language transition guide.
  - [Jogakbo — Trans Human Rights Group](https://transgender.or.kr/)
  - [Solidarity for LGBT Human Rights of Korea](https://lgbtpride.or.kr/), [SOGI Law & Policy Research Group](https://sogilaw.org/)

## 2. Psychiatric Evaluation

- Visit a trans-competent psychiatric clinic / hospital department for one or more sessions of interviews and psychological testing.
- Obtain a certificate of **F64.0 (Gender Identity Disorder)** or ICD-11 **HA60 (Gender Incongruence)** — used downstream for HRT, SRS, and legal correction.
- Out-of-pocket cost: ~₩300,000 at private clinics, ₩400,000–500,000 at university hospitals.

See: [HRT Overview (South Korea)]({{< ref "/docs/hrt/korea/overview" >}})

## 3. Hormone Therapy (HRT)

- Bring the diagnosis to endocrinology, urology, OB/GYN, or general practice — or stay at a clinic that combines psychiatric diagnosis with HRT prescribing.
- Baseline labs (LFTs, renal panel, hormones, lipids) precede first dose.
- Monitoring typically every **3 months in year one**, then every 6–12 months.
- Entirely out-of-pocket.

→ [HRT Overview (South Korea)]({{< ref "/docs/hrt/korea/overview" >}})

## 4. Optional Procedures

Choose based on your goals and body:

- **Voice training / voice feminization surgery**
- **Facial feminization surgery (FFS)** — forehead, nose, jaw, chin, trachea, etc. Korea's plastic-surgery sector is well developed.
- **Hair removal** — laser (multiple sessions) and electrolysis.
- **Breast augmentation (BA)** — often deferred until 1–2 years on HRT to evaluate natural development.

## 5. Sex Reassignment Surgery (SRS)

- A documented period of HRT and psychiatric assessment usually precedes SRS.
- Common techniques in Korea: penile inversion (PI), PI + scrotal flap, sigmoid colon, robotic peritoneal.
- Self-pay; total package commonly ₩15M–40M.

→ [SRS Overview (South Korea)]({{< ref "/docs/srs/korea/overview" >}}), [Dong-A]({{< ref "/docs/srs/korea/dong-a" >}}), [Kangdong Sacred Heart]({{< ref "/docs/srs/korea/kangdong" >}})

## 6. Military Service (where applicable)

- As long as the Family Relations Register lists you as male, you are subject to military examination.
- The 2024 revised *Rules for Military Physical Examinations* treat **≥ 6 months of HRT** as objective evidence of social gender identity.
- Completion of external genital reconstruction + legal correction exempts from examination; surgery alone (without legal correction) typically yields a Grade 5 (wartime reservist) result.
- If a notice may arrive while your case is pending, plan a strategy with your lawyer and advocacy group in advance.

→ [SRS Overview (Military section)]({{< ref "/docs/srs/korea/overview#military" >}})

## 7. Legal Gender Correction

- Petition your residential family court (or competent district court) for *gender correction permission*.
- The Supreme Court's Family Relations Registration Directive No. 550 is the operating reference; **after the February 2024 revision**, external genital reconstruction and sterilization are reclassified as "reference points" rather than approval criteria.
- Outcomes still vary substantially by judge — consult a lawyer or one of the Korean organizations listed above before filing.
- After the order is granted, register the correction at the city/ward office within one month, then update ID, driver's licence, passport, NHIS, etc. sequentially.

→ [Legal Gender Recognition in South Korea]({{< ref "/docs/srs/korea/legal-id" >}})

## 8. After Transition

- Maintain follow-up labs (bloods, bone density, cardiovascular risk).
- Update name and gender at work, school, banks, telecoms, etc. Some institutions cooperate with name/display changes even before legal correction is complete.
- Allow time for relationships with family, partners, and children to adjust.

## For Foreign Trans Women

- Korean medical services (HRT, SRS) are accessible to foreigners with an alien-registration card or passport.
- Korean Family Relations Registration applies only to Korean nationals; foreign-national legal gender change must be filed under your home country's laws.
- For records to be usable abroad, obtain English-language documents and arrange notarization / apostille.
